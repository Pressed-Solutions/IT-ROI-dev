Capable of great things

PPM ideaBoss is a SharePoint web interface for ideation that directly embeds into CA's Clarity PPM. Ideation for CA Clarity Demand Management most exciting benefit, well in our book anyways, is that it allows your organization to maximize their capacity to spark innovation by amplifying the voice of their internal personnel and external customers throughout their product and service offerings. Successful idea management for Clarity PPM allows everyone to tell you about your offerings or policies on their own terms; what is valuable or useless, what could be an incredible addition, or what would make them turn to your competitor. Now, you can ask them — all of them, easily and quickly, and they can respond just as simply, and they don't have to be Clarity PPM licensed users.

Effective Demand Management planning for CA Clarity PPM requires that all ideas to be put in front of an idea management team for review. This ensures company goals are properly aligned before the idea is escalated to a CA Clarity PPM idea or better yet, transformed to a CA Clarity PPM project.

Use IT-ROI's ideaBoss SharePoint to Clarity PPM Connector to:

 
  • Align PPM strategies with resources. Maximize your capacity to drive innovation.
  • Amplify the voice of your customer throughout your offerings.
  • Attain a complete picture of "what's hot and what's not" .
  • Exponentially extend the reach of brainstorming across the global enterprise and marketplace.
  • Objectively measure market and management demand to ensure you deliver high value offerings.
  • Ensure ideas can be evaluated against strategy, resources, and cost.
